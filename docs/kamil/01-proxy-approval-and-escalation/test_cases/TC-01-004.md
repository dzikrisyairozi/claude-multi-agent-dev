# TC-01-004: Proxy approve workflow completes successfully

## Type
happy-path

## Preconditions
- An escalated submission at step 2 of a 3-step approval route
- Step 2 was assigned to "Erika" who did not act
- User is logged in as admin "Rita"

## Steps
1. Navigate to Dashboard > Escalations tab
2. Click "Take Action" on the escalated submission
3. Click "Proxy Approve"
4. Review the proxy warning ("You will approve on behalf of Erika")
5. Click "Continue"

## Expected Result
- The submission's step 2 is marked as approved
- `approval_request_step_approvals.acted_by` = Rita's ID
- `approval_request_step_approvals.proxy_approved_by` = Rita's ID
- Submission advances to step 3 (next approver is notified)
- If step 2 was the last step, submission is fully approved
- `is_escalated` resets to `false` after proxy approval
- The submission disappears from the Escalations tab
- Success toast notification appears

## Priority
critical
